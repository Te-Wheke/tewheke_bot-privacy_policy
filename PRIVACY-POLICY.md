Privacy Policy for Karanga (@TeWhekeBot)

Effective date: 27 May 2026

Karanga (“the Bot”, “we”, “us”) is a privacy-focused Telegram bot operated by Te Whare Raurunga. The Bot may use privacy-enhancing infrastructure, including Tor-based network routing, as part of its backend operations. However, use of Tor or similar infrastructure does not by itself make interactions with the Bot fully anonymous.

1. Scope
This Privacy Policy explains what information Karanga may receive through Telegram, how that information is used, and how users can contact the operator.

This policy applies only to Karanga (@TeWhekeBot). It does not replace Telegram’s own Privacy Policy, which governs Telegram’s platform and services.

2. Information We May Receive
When you interact with the Bot, we may receive Telegram-provided account and interaction data necessary for the Bot to function, such as:
- your Telegram user ID
- your username, if you have one
- your display name and profile information made available by Telegram
- messages, commands, and files you send to the Bot
- technical interaction data required to operate, secure, and maintain the Bot

3. How We Use Information
We use information only to:
- operate the Bot
- respond to user inputs and commands
- maintain security, stability, and abuse prevention
- debug, improve, or restore the service

We do not sell your data.

4. Privacy and Anonymity Statement
Karanga is designed to be privacy-focused. Backend network routing may use Tor or similar privacy-enhancing infrastructure.

However, Karanga should not be described or relied upon as completely anonymous. Telegram may provide identifying account data to the Bot when you interact with it, and no system can guarantee absolute anonymity or confidentiality unless explicitly engineered, verified, and stated with precision.

Do not send sensitive personal information unless you are comfortable doing so.

5. Data Retention
We aim to keep data only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, and maintain the Bot.

Data may be deleted earlier at our discretion. Some data may remain temporarily in logs, backups, or Telegram systems outside our direct control.

6. Data Sharing
We do not intentionally sell or rent user data.

We may disclose limited information only where necessary:
- to run hosting or infrastructure used for the Bot
- to investigate abuse, fraud, or security incidents
- to comply with applicable law or valid legal process

7. Third Parties
Karanga operates through Telegram. Your use of Telegram is also subject to Telegram’s own terms and privacy practices.

If the Bot later connects to external services, this policy may be updated.

8. Security
We take reasonable steps to limit access to data and secure the Bot’s infrastructure. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Children
Karanga is not intended for anyone prohibited from using Telegram under applicable rules or local law.

10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version published for the Bot will apply.
